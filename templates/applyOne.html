How long will it take ?

Apply online and your account could be open in less than 10 minutes.

If you are an existing NatWest customer you can apply even faster if you know a few details.

To start your application, we need some information that helps us to confirm your identity.

We can usually confirm your identity online and open your new account instantly. This means you won't need to visit a branch or post any documents to us.

To do this, we need information from a current debit or credit card AND one of these three things:

  • UK driving licence (we can't accept licences registered to addresses in Northern Ireland, Jersey, the Isle of Man or Gibraltar)
  • OR passport
  • OR EU identity card

Get your card and documents ready now to save time.
